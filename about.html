As one of the industry’s most booked and respected party bands, The Funcation Bad now have a reputation within the Greenwich and Lewisham area for being the best at what they do. Members in the band have also performed alongside the likes of Joss Stone, John Newman, John Legend, Jack Savoretti, Florence and the Machine, Emeli Sande, Lily Allen and Maxi Priest.

Available from a 7 piece up to an almighty 20 Piece Orchestra, The Funcation Band have the ability to tailor the band line up to cater for any clients requests. With an extensive ever growing repertoire of Funk, Soul, Disco and Pop anthems, the band have something for everyone in their live show. The Funcation Band are available for weddings, corporate events, awards nights, Jewish celebrations, charity balls and any other event you can think of.

The Function Band are at the top of their game when it comes to providing live music for events. The band sound fantastic, look great and are an ideal choice for any event – if you want an amazing party atmosphere and a packed dance floor all night long.
